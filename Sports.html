Sports Programs

Building teamwork, discipline, and healthy bodies alongside sharp minds.

What We Offer

Physical education is core to our approach at Ministers' Touch Schools. Our sports programs teach students discipline, teamwork, and resilience — values that carry over into the classroom and beyond.

Team Sport

Football (Soccer)

Junior and senior teams train weekly and compete in inter-school leagues each term.

Team Sport

Basketball

Boys' and girls' basketball teams with coaching for beginners through to competitive players.

Track & Field

Athletics

Sprints, relays, and field events, with our annual Sports Day as the season highlight.

Team Sport

Netball

A long-standing favorite among our senior girls, with regular fixtures against neighboring schools.

Individual

Swimming

Beginner and advanced swimming lessons at partner facilities, building water safety and fitness.

Recreation

Table Tennis & Chess

Low-impact clubs that build focus, patience, and strategic thinking for students of all ages.
